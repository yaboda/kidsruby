KidsRuby lets you program the Sphero (http://gosphero.com) robotic ball.

If you are on OSX or Linux, you will need to know the three letter device ID that is the "name" of your Sphero, for example, "GBY".

If you are on Windows, you will need to know which COM port has been assigned to the Bluetooth connection to your Sphero, for example, "COM6".

Make sure you change the parameter to the Sphero.start method to whatever is needed to connect to your own Sphero.

To start your Sphero robot, once you have the correct connection info, and have typed in your program, just click on the "Run" button. It might take a moment to connect.
